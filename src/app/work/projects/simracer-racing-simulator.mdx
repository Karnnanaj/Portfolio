## Overview

Started SimRacer, a company dedicated to designing and building sim racing setups using open-source hardware components. The company specializes in custom-engineered racing simulations with precision pedal configurations and real-time auto calibration.

## Key Products & Features

- **High Resolution Encoding**: Precise steering angle detection
- **Leather Grip**: Premium rim construction with ergonomic design
- **Programmable Buttons**: Customizable controls for various racing sims
- **USB Interface**: Plug-and-play compatibility with popular racing platforms
