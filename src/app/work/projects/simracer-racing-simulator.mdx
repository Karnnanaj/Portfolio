## Overview

Started SimRacer, a company dedicated to designing and building premium sim racing setups using open-source hardware components. The company specializes in custom-engineered racing simulations with authentic force feedback steering systems and precision pedal configurations, targeting both enthusiasts and professional racing teams.

## Key Products & Features

### Steering Wheel System
- **Direct Drive Motor**: High-torque, low-latency force feedback
- **High Resolution Encoding**: Precise steering angle detection
- **Leather Grip**: Premium rim construction with ergonomic design
- **Programmable Buttons**: Customizable controls for various racing sims
- **Force Feedback**: Realistic road texture and impact feedback
- **USB Interface**: Plug-and-play compatibility with popular racing platforms

### Pedal System
- **Load Cell Technology**: Independent brake load measurement
- **Linear Potentiometers**: Throttle and clutch control
- **Adjustable Resistance**: Customizable pedal feel and travel
- **Compact Footprint**: Space-efficient design for various setups
- **Modular Construction**: Easy upgrades and component replacement
- **Cable Management**: Organized wiring for professional appearance

### Frame & Structure
- **Aluminum Profile Construction**: Lightweight yet rigid frame
- **Adjustable Geometry**: Cockpit height and angle customization
- **Tool-Free Assembly**: Quick setup and configuration
- **Cable Routing**: Integrated cable management system
- **Stability**: Low center of gravity for reduced vibration
- **Scalable Design**: Expandable for additional components

## Design Philosophy

SimRacer's approach combines professional-grade components with open-source principles:

1. **Affordability**: Cost-effective alternative to commercial systems
2. **Quality**: Components sourced from reliable manufacturers
3. **Customization**: Modular design allows user personalization
4. **Community**: Open-source designs encourage community contributions
5. **Sustainability**: Repairable and upgradeable for long-term use

## Hardware Integration

- **Compatible Games**: iRacing, Assetto Corsa, rFactor 2, BeamNG, and more
- **Driver Software**: Custom drivers for optimal force feedback tuning
- **Configuration Utility**: User-friendly software for calibration and customization
- **Firmware Updates**: Regular updates for compatibility and performance improvements

## Custom USB Controller

Built custom firmware for real-time force feedback control:
- **Custom Firmware**: Pi Pico microcontroller running custom USB controller firmware
- **4 Analog Inputs**: Steering, throttle, brake, and clutch channels
- **12 Digital Inputs**: Buttons for game controls and function mapping
- **Real-time Processing**: `<5ms` response time for critical inputs
- **Full HID Compliance**: Works seamlessly with racing simulators
